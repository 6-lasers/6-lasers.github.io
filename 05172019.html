Overall Verdict (tl;dr)

Special Box is one of the best at this price point. Not as good as the Ultra Box, but much better than the average 480-coin box.

Great Box is less terrible than normal but still worse than any of the other deals available.

Ultra Box has 20 passes. If you like passes that's probably all you need to hear, but the "bonus" items are good too--4 Super Incubators and 5 Star Pieces.

Adventure Box, as usual, has a very consistent deal. The current deal is roughly tied for best Adventure Box yet.

See table at the bottom of the post for justifications of item valuation.

If you only care about Raid Passes and don't want Incubators, the best deal is 74.0 coins/pass, from the Ultra Box.
